The Artist

About

Yakir Moskovich — known as KOI BOI — has been crafting deep, cosmic psytrance since 2005. Born and based in Israel, his sound blends hypnotic basslines, intricate layering, and otherworldly atmospheres that have earned him releases on some of the scene's most respected labels.

Under the visual alias O00010O, he extends his art into real-time generative visuals, TouchDesigner patches, and live VJ performances — creating immersive audio-visual experiences that take the psytrance journey to another dimension.
